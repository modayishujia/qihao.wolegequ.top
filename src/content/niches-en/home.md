# Home & Interior Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Home covers interior design, home products, space makeovers, and organization. It's highly practical with strong monetization potential.

### Core Audience

- Homeowners and renters aged 25-40
- Interested in improving living spaces
- Have renovation or decoration needs

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Renovation Tips | What to avoid when renovating | Launch |
| Product Picks | Home product recommendations | Growth |
| Makeovers | Rental/room transformations | Growth |
| Organization | Storage solutions and tools | Established |
| Style Guides | Interior design styles | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Before/After**: "Rental apartment transformation"
- **Products**: "Home items that boost happiness"
- **Renovation**: "10 costly renovation mistakes"
- **Organization**: "Small space storage solutions"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Before/after comparisons are the strongest content
2. Include specific product links and prices
3. Real living spaces > staged photos
4. Share budgets and solutions

## Monetization

- Home product affiliates
- Brand collaborations
- Interior design consulting
- Live commerce
- Paid renovation guides

## Notes

- Only recommend products you've actually used
- Include safety reminders for renovations
- Keep price information accurate
