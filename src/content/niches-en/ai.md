# AI Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

AI is the hottest niche of 2024-2025, covering AI tools, AI art, AI writing, AI coding, and more.

### Core Audience

- Professionals interested in new technology
- Creators wanting to boost productivity with AI
- Investors and industry professionals following AI

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Tool Tutorials | How to use AI tools | Launch |
| Case Studies | AI-generated work showcase | Growth |
| Comparisons | AI vs traditional methods | Established |
| Industry Insights | AI trend analysis | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Tool Reviews**: "10 best AI tools you need to try"
- **Tutorials**: "Write a blog post in 5 minutes with AI"
- **Showcases**: "How realistic AI-generated images have become"
- **Industry Thoughts**: "Which jobs will AI replace?"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Stay on top of the latest AI developments
2. Be an early adopter and reviewer of new tools
3. Use before/after comparisons to showcase AI capabilities
4. Build an "AI tools encyclopedia" series

## Monetization

- AI tool affiliate commissions
- Paid tutorials/courses
- AI art/writing services
- Corporate AI training

## Notes

- AI changes fast — keep content updated
- Be aware of copyright issues with AI-generated content
- Don't overhype AI — stay objective
