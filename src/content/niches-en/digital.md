# Digital Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

The digital niche covers phones, tablets, laptops, headphones, cameras, and other consumer electronics. It's one of the most popular niches on content platforms.

### Core Audience

- Primarily males aged 18-35
- Interested in value-for-money and new product launches
- Tend to check reviews before purchasing

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Unboxing | First impressions of new products | Launch |
| Comparisons | Head-to-head product comparisons | Growth |
| Tips & Tutorials | In-depth usage guides | Established |
| Recommendations | Scenario-based picks | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Trending**: Follow product launches and industry news
- **Pain Points**: "Best headphones under $50"
- **Scenarios**: "College student tech essentials"
- **Debates**: "iPhone vs Android real-world experience"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Post consistently (daily or every other day)
2. Maintain a unified visual style for brand recognition
3. Engage actively in comments (reply to first 20)
4. Ride trends but add your unique perspective

## Monetization

- Affiliate product links
- Sponsored content
- Paid buying guides
- Live streaming commerce

## Notes

- Stay objective — avoid overhyping products
- Follow platform advertising rules
- Build your own review framework
