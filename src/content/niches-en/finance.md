# Finance Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Finance covers personal finance, investing, side hustles, and financial planning. Users have high willingness to pay.

### Core Audience

- Professionals aged 22-40
- Financial awareness but lack knowledge
- Want to increase income sources

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Finance Basics | Fundamental money concepts | Launch |
| Budgeting | Expense management methods | Growth |
| Side Hustles | Ways to earn extra income | Growth |
| Investing | Stock/fund basics | Established |
| Financial Planning | Long-term money goals | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Basics**: "How to manage money on a $3K salary"
- **Side Hustles**: "10 side hustles for full-time workers"
- **Saving Tips**: "How I saved $10K in one year"
- **Investing**: "Beginner's guide to index fund investing"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Use specific numbers to attract attention
2. Make content actionable for ordinary people
3. Share real results (with compliance disclaimers)
4. Build a "finance mentor" persona

## Monetization

- Paid finance courses
- Paid communities/memberships
- Financial tool affiliates
- Brand collaborations
- 1-on-1 consulting

## Notes

- Don't recommend specific stocks or funds
- Never promise returns — include risk disclaimers
- Follow financial content regulations
