# Photography Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Photography covers portraits, landscapes, food, street photography, and post-processing. It's highly visual and platform-friendly.

### Core Audience

- Photography enthusiasts and hobbyists
- People who want to improve their visual content
- Professional and semi-professional photographers

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Photo Sharing | Best work showcase | Launch |
| Tutorials | Shooting techniques | Growth |
| Editing Guides | Post-processing tips | Growth |
| Gear Reviews | Camera and lens reviews | Established |
| Behind the Scenes | Creative process | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Tutorials**: "Master golden hour photography"
- **Editing**: "Lightroom mobile editing workflow"
- **Gear**: "Best cameras for beginners in 2025"
- **Inspiration**: "30-day photo challenge"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Consistent visual style across all posts
2. Share before/after editing comparisons
3. Teach specific, actionable techniques
4. Engage with the photography community

## Monetization

- Camera gear affiliates
- Preset/filter sales
- Photography workshops
- Brand collaborations
- Print sales

## Notes

- Always credit locations and models
- Be transparent about editing extent
- Respect copyright of others' work
