# Lifestyle Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Lifestyle is one of the broadest niches, covering daily life, home decor, fashion, food, and travel. It's beginner-friendly.

### Core Audience

- Primarily females aged 18-30
- Aspire to quality living
- Enjoy authentic life sharing

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Daily Sharing | Real life documentation | Launch |
| Product Picks | Lifestyle product recommendations | Growth |
| Life Hacks | Organization, cooking, saving tips | Established |
| Life Philosophy | Minimalism, sustainable living | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Home Makeover**: "Rental apartment before & after"
- **Daily Vlog**: "A day in my life as a solo dweller"
- **Product Picks**: "Little things that boost happiness"
- **Budget Tips**: "Living well on a budget"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Authenticity > perfection
2. Find your unique tag and memory point
3. Chat in comments to build emotional connection
4. Ride trending topics naturally

## Monetization

- Product showcases
- Brand collaborations
- Live commerce
- Own brand/shop

## Notes

- Don't over-package — authenticity matters
- Protect personal privacy
- Keep content positive and uplifting
