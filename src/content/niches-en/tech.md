# Tech Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

The tech niche covers software, hardware, internet products, and technology news. It appeals to tech enthusiasts and professionals.

### Core Audience

- Tech-savvy professionals and enthusiasts
- People looking for productivity tools
- Early adopters of new technology

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Tool Reviews | Software/app reviews | Launch |
| Tutorials | How-to guides | Growth |
| Industry News | Tech trend analysis | Growth |
| Productivity | Workflow optimization | Established |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Tool Reviews**: "10 best productivity apps in 2025"
- **Tutorials**: "Complete guide to Obsidian"
- **Industry Analysis**: "Why Apple is changing strategy"
- **Comparisons**: "Notion vs Obsidian vs Logseq"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Be the first to cover new tools
2. Create comprehensive, bookmark-worthy guides
3. Use screen recordings for clarity
4. Build authority in a specific sub-niche

## Monetization

- Software affiliate programs
- Sponsored content
- Paid tutorials/courses
- Consulting services

## Notes

- Keep up with rapid industry changes
- Verify information before publishing
- Balance depth with accessibility
