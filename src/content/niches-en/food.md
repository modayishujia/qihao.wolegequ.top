# Food Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Food is one of the easiest niches to start, covering home cooking, baking, restaurant reviews, and kitchen tools.

### Core Audience

- All age groups
- Home cooks and food lovers
- People looking for recipe inspiration

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Recipes | Easy-to-follow dishes | Launch |
| Restaurant Reviews | Local/travel food spots | Growth |
| Baking Tutorials | Desserts and bread | Growth |
| Kitchen Tools | Must-have cooking gear | Established |
| Food Reviews | Snack/fast food reviews | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Quick Meals**: "A fancy dish in 10 minutes"
- **Reviews**: "Hidden gem restaurants only locals know"
- **Baking**: "Zero-fail sponge cake recipe"
- **Taste Tests**: "Which supermarket ready meal is best?"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. High-quality food photography is essential
2. Clear steps that beginners can follow
3. Use specific numbers and times in titles
4. Create seasonal/holiday content

## Monetization

- Kitchen tool affiliates
- Brand collaborations
- Paid recipe books/courses
- Live commerce
- Own food brand

## Notes

- Food safety first — note expiration dates
- Be honest in restaurant reviews
- Include allergen warnings
