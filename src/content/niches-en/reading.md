# Reading & Growth Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Reading covers book recommendations, reading notes, personal growth, and thinking methods. It attracts high-quality users with clear monetization paths.

### Core Audience

- Knowledge-oriented individuals aged 20-40
- Seeking self-improvement
- Willing to pay for knowledge

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Book Lists | Themed reading lists | Launch |
| Book Notes | Key takeaways from books | Growth |
| Thinking Models | Mental models and frameworks | Growth |
| Growth Stories | Personal development journeys | Established |
| Productivity | Learning and work efficiency | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Book Lists**: "10 books that changed my life"
- **Notes**: "Key insights from Principles by Ray Dalio"
- **Thinking**: "Charlie Munger's 100 mental models"
- **Productivity**: "5 methods for effective reading"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. High-quality notes and lists are core content
2. Present knowledge visually
3. Establish "book blogger" identity
4. Regular reading challenges for engagement

## Monetization

- Book affiliate commissions
- Paid reading communities
- Knowledge courses
- Brand collaborations
- Writing/column opportunities

## Notes

- Respect intellectual property
- Don't oversimplify complex ideas
- Avoid clickbait — maintain depth
