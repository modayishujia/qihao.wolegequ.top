# Beauty Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Beauty is one of the most popular niches, covering skincare, makeup, beauty treatments, and personal care.

### Core Audience

- Primarily females aged 18-35
- Interested in ingredients and efficacy
- Check real reviews before purchasing

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Product Picks | Affordable/premium recommendations | Launch |
| Ingredient Science | Skincare ingredient breakdowns | Growth |
| Tutorials | Everyday/special occasion makeup | Growth |
| Comparisons | Head-to-head product tests | Established |
| Skin Diary | Real skin transformation records | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Dupes**: "Affordable alternatives to luxury products"
- **Ingredients**: "Does niacinamide really brighten skin?"
- **Reviews**: "30-day honest review"
- **Tutorials**: "5-minute everyday makeup look"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Unified visual style with high aesthetics
2. Real experience > hard advertising
3. Follow trending products and beauty trends
4. Create a personal signature tag

## Monetization

- Brand collaborations
- Affiliate product links
- Live commerce
- Own brand/co-branded products
- Paid skincare consultations

## Notes

- No false claims or misleading content
- Add disclaimers for skincare results
- Follow platform rules for beauty content
