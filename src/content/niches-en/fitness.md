# Fitness Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Fitness covers strength training, cardio, yoga, running, and sports equipment. It has a wide and engaged audience.

### Core Audience

- Males and females aged 18-40
- Focused on body management and health
- Have clear goals (fat loss/muscle gain/toning)

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Exercise Tutorials | Proper form demonstrations | Launch |
| Training Plans | Weekly/monthly programs | Growth |
| Diet Plans | Nutrition for fat loss/muscle gain | Growth |
| Transformations | Before/after comparisons | Established |
| Gear Reviews | Equipment recommendations | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Home Workouts**: "Get abs without a gym membership"
- **Beginner Guide**: "10 exercises every beginner should know"
- **Diet Plans**: "What to eat during a cut"
- **Progress**: "My 100-day transformation"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Physical transformation is your strongest credential
2. Make content follow-along friendly
3. Post consistently to build habits
4. Create challenges for engagement

## Monetization

- Sports gear affiliates
- Online coaching/programs
- Paid fitness communities
- Supplement partnerships
- Brand collaborations

## Notes

- Emphasize safety and proper form
- Don't promote extreme dieting
- Advise users to adjust for their level
