# Gaming Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Gaming covers game guides, esports news, game reviews, and streaming. The audience is young and highly engaged.

### Core Audience

- Primarily males aged 16-30
- Active gamers
- Follow new games and esports events

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Game Guides | Walkthrough and tips | Launch |
| Game Reviews | New game evaluations | Growth |
| Esports News | Tournament analysis | Growth |
| Funny Clips | Epic and hilarious moments | Established |
| Gear Reviews | Gaming equipment reviews | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Guides**: "Beginner's ranking up guide"
- **Reviews**: "Top 10 games worth playing in 2025"
- **Esports**: "Worlds Championship recap"
- **Funny**: "Most ridiculous game bugs"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Follow trending games and events
2. High-quality editing and commentary
3. Build a personal style (funny/pro/educational)
4. Run both short videos and live streams

## Monetization

- Game promotion partnerships
- Gaming gear affiliates
- Live stream donations
- Paid coaching/duo sessions
- Brand collaborations

## Notes

- Avoid unmarked spoilers
- Respect game copyright
- Don't promote cheats or hacks
