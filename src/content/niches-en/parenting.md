# Parenting Niche Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Parenting covers pregnancy, newborns, early childhood education, and family activities. Trust is crucial, but engagement and monetization potential are strong.

### Core Audience

- Parents aged 25-40
- Interested in evidence-based parenting
- Willing to invest in their children

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Parenting Diary | Real parenting stories | Launch |
| Product Reviews | Baby and child product reviews | Growth |
| Early Education | Home learning activities | Growth |
| Parenting Science | Evidence-based parenting tips | Established |
| Family Activities | Fun things to do together | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Product Picks**: "Essential items for new parents"
- **Activities**: "Early education games you can do at home"
- **Lessons Learned**: "Parenting mistakes I made"
- **Recipes": "Baby food introduction guide by month"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Share real experiences to build connection
2. Answer questions actively in comments
3. Build trust as an experienced parent
4. Share money-saving tips

## Monetization

- Baby product affiliates
- Brand collaborations
- Paid parenting courses
- Parenting communities
- Live commerce

## Notes

- Don't spread unverified parenting advice
- Protect children's privacy
- Follow platform rules for baby products
