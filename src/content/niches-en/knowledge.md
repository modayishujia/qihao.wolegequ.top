# Knowledge Monetization Growth Guide

## Niche Positioning

Knowledge monetization covers paid courses, paid communities, consulting, and expertise sharing. It has the highest per-user value.

### Core Audience

- Professionals with expertise to share
- People seeking to monetize their knowledge
- Lifelong learners willing to pay for quality content

## Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Types

| Type | Description | Stage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Free Knowledge | Share valuable insights for free | Launch |
| Case Studies | Success stories and results | Growth |
| Methodology | Structured frameworks | Established |
| Paid Content | Courses and premium resources | Monetization |

### 2. Topic Ideas

- **Free Value**: "5 lessons from my $100K side hustle"
- **Case Studies**: "How I grew from 0 to 100K followers"
- **Frameworks**: "The content creation framework I use"
- **Behind the Scenes**: "How I create my courses"

### 3. Growth Tips

1. Give away 80% of knowledge for free
2. Use results as social proof
3. Build email list from day one
4. Create a clear content-to-product funnel

## Monetization

- Online courses
- Paid communities/memberships
- Consulting/coaching
- E-books and digital products
- Speaking engagements

## Notes

- Don't gatekeep basic knowledge
- Deliver real value in paid products
- Build genuine expertise before teaching
